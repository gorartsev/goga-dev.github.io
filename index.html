About Me

I’m a Level Designer focused on creating readable, engaging environments for games. I work with Unity and Unreal Engine 5 — from mobile hybrid-casual levels and VR simulators to PC/console-style atmospheric worlds like Temple of Mind.

My experience includes working at 24 Play LLC (NDA), designing VR training environments for Khalifa University, hybrid-casual levels and virtual tours.
