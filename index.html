About

What I focus on as a Level Designer.
I’m a Level Designer and UX/UI Designer focused on designing clear, atmospheric spaces that guide players naturally through gameplay, pacing, and mood. I work in Unity and Unreal Engine, creating levels for hybrid-casual mobile games and more cinematic PC scenes.

I enjoy building spaces where the environment itself teaches the player — through visual language, rhythm, and carefully placed points of interest.

Strengths:
• Level pacing and “flow”
• Readability & navigation cues
• Camera and path composition
• UX-driven thinking inside 3D environments

Tools:
Unity (URP), Unreal Engine 5, Figma, Photoshop, Miro, Blender (basic).
