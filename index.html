ZoneCity Runner
ZoneCity is a runner prototype built around exploring a compact city surrounded by dense forest. The player moves through streets, alleys and small green areas to find and collect 10 hidden artifacts. The flow is shaped by the contrast between urban structure and nature: clean lane patterns, clear visual markers and rhythmic environmental changes maintain readability and keep the experience engaging along the entire route.

About

I’m a Level Designer and UX/UI specialist focused on creating readable, engaging spaces for games and simulations. I work primarily with Unity and Unreal Engine 5.

My experience includes designing production-ready levels at 24 Play LLC, VR training spaces for Khalifa University and 3D-infused UX for an e-commerce platform.
