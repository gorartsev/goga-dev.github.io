About

How I think about level design and what I bring to a team.
I’m a Level Designer and UX/UI specialist focused on creating readable, engaging spaces for games and simulations. I work primarily with Unity and Unreal Engine 5, building landscapes, environments and biomes for mobile hybrid/casual titles, PC and console-style projects, VR experiences and interactive tours.

My experience includes designing production-ready levels at 24 Play LLC, VR training spaces for Khalifa University, virtual tour environments for an NDA project and 3D-infused UX for an e-commerce platform at Green Cities. I rely on analytics, playtests and clear visual language to iterate levels until they feel intuitive and fun, and I’m actively growing towards designing full levels and experiences for PC and console games.

Strengths:
• Gameplay flow, patterns & balance
• Level readability and navigation cues
• VR-specific UX and comfort
• Working with feedback, analytics and tight deadlines

Tools:
Unity, Unreal Engine 5, Figma, Adobe Photoshop / Illustrator, Miro, AI-assisted tools.

Languages:
English – Intermediate, Ukrainian – Native, Russian – Native.
