About

How I think about level design and what I bring to a team.
I’m a 3D Level Designer and UX/UI specialist focused on creating readable, engaging spaces for games and simulations. I work primarily with Unity and Unreal Engine 5, building landscapes, environments and biomes for mobile hybrid/casual titles, VR projects and interactive tours.

My experience includes designing production-ready levels at 24 Play LLC, VR training spaces for Khalifa University, virtual tour environments for an NDA project and 3D-infused UX for an e-commerce platform at Green Cities. I rely on analytics, playtests and clear visual language to iterate levels until they feel intuitive and fun.

Strengths:
• Gameplay flow, patterns & balance
• Level readability and navigation cues
• VR-specific UX and comfort
• Working with feedback, analytics and tight deadlines

Tools:
Unity, Unreal Engine 5, Figma, Adobe Photoshop / Illustrator, Miro, AI-assisted tools.

Languages:
English – Intermediate, Ukrainian – Native, Russian – Native.
